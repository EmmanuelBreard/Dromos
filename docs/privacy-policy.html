Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 14, 2026

Dromos ("we", "our", "the app") is a triathlon training planner for iOS. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we handle it.

1. Data We Collect

Account information

  • Email address and password (for authentication)

Personal information (provided during onboarding)

  • Biological sex, date of birth, and weight

Training preferences (provided during onboarding)

  • Race objective and target date
  • Target finish time
  • Performance metrics: VMA, CSS, and FTP (optional)
  • Years of experience and current weekly training hours
  • Weekly sport availability (swim, bike, run days) and daily training durations

Generated data

  • Your personalized training plan, including weekly phases and individual sessions, generated by our system based on the information above

Technical data

  • IP address and standard server logs, collected automatically when you use the app

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account
  • To generate a personalized triathlon training plan using AI
  • To display your plan and profile within the app

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to providing the Dromos service.

3. AI-Generated Plans

Your onboarding data (goals, metrics, availability) is sent to an AI model on our server to generate your training plan. This processing happens server-side. The AI does not retain your data after generating the plan.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure, which acts as our data processor. No other third-party services receive your data.

5. Data Security

  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • All data is protected by row-level security policies, ensuring you can only access your own data
  • All communication between the app and our servers is encrypted via HTTPS

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all associated data — your profile, training plans, and sessions — is permanently and automatically deleted. There is no recovery after deletion.

7. Your Rights

You can:

  • Access and update your personal data via the Profile screen in the app
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Contact us with any questions about your data

8. Children's Privacy

Dromos is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Analytics and Tracking

Dromos does not use analytics services, crash reporting tools, advertising SDKs, or any form of user tracking.

10. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
